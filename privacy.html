Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Mello mobile application and any related services operated by Iris Maria Arteaga Guzman ("Service Provider").

Information Collection and Use

The Application does not collect, log, or store personal information when you download or use it. Registration is not required. When connected to the internet, technical protocol data such as an ephemeral IP address may be transmitted to facilitate network connectivity, but this information is not retained or used for tracking purposes.

Location Information

The Application does not collect precise real-time location information from your device.

Third-Party Access

Because the Application does not collect personal information, no data is shared with third parties.

Opt-Out Rights

Since the Application does not collect personal information through normal use, uninstalling it removes the Application from your device. If you voluntarily provide information by contacting the Service Provider, you may request deletion of that information by emailing legal@mellotheapp.com.

Children's Privacy

The Application is not intended for children under 16 years of age, or such higher age as required by applicable law. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect data from children or market to them. If personal information is voluntarily provided through other means, parental or guardian consent may be required where permitted by law.

Security

Because the Application does not collect personal data, the risk of personal data exposure is minimal. However, no system is completely secure, and the Service Provider implements reasonable safeguards to protect any information it may hold.

Data Breach Notification

Since the Application does not collect personal data through normal use, the risk of a data breach affecting personal information is minimal. If a breach occurs involving information voluntarily provided by a user, the Service Provider will provide notification as required by applicable law.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be posted with an updated effective date. Where required by law, consent will be obtained before material changes take effect. Previous versions may be requested by contacting the Service Provider.

Effective Date

This Privacy Policy is effective as of June 17, 2026.

Your Consent

If you voluntarily provide information and processing is based on consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time without affecting processing that occurred before withdrawal.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the Service Provider's practices, please contact: support@mellotheapp.com
